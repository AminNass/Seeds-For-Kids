Sow the seeds of healthy eating in young children

At Seeds for Kids, we believe that teaching children to enjoy healthy food begins with taste education. Only by expanding their taste range can we increase their acceptance of new foods. That’s why taste education is at the heart of everything we do.
We design personalised programmes that encourage young children to explore, enjoy, and embrace new flavours. By nurturing their natural curiosity and sense of adventure, we help them discover the joy of tasting a wide variety of healthy ingredients, from fruits and vegetables to whole grains, laying the foundation for lifelong healthy eating habits.
Taste education is also a powerful vehicle for sustainability: every bite can change the world. When children learn to love diverse, plant-based foods, they help grow a sustainable planet rich in biodiversity.

Founded in 2017 by Raffaella, Seeds for Kids is built on the belief that every child can learn to love wholesome, healthy, and sustainable food.
